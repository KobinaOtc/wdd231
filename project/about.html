Our Story & Mission

Learn more about the team of educators and parents behind the Kookofio app.

Empowering Little Communicators

Kookofio was born from a simple idea: to make early speech development fun and accessible for every child. We are a small team of passionate educators, designers, and parents who believe that every child deserves the confidence to express themselves. Our mission is to provide an engaging, play-based tool that supports this vital stage of growth, transforming a challenging journey into a joyful adventure.
